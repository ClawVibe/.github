## What is ClawVibe?

ClawVibe is a native iOS app that gives you an always-on voice interface to your OpenClaw agents. Talk to your AI hands-free — from your iPhone, CarPlay dashboard, Apple Watch, or lock screen.

### Key Features

- **Always-On Voice** — Continuous conversation with silence detection. No buttons, no wake words. Just talk.
- **On-Device Speech** — Speech recognition and neural TTS run entirely on your device. Your voice never leaves your phone.
- **CarPlay & Apple Watch** — Full CarPlay dashboard, Watch companion, and lock screen controls. Hands-free everywhere.
- **Natural Conversation** — Interrupt mid-sentence, both ways. TTS plays instantly, sentence by sentence.
- **Speaker Identity** — Voice fingerprinting detects who is speaking for personalised context.
- **Multi-Agent** — Switch between OpenClaw agents on the fly.
- **Location-Aware** — GPS context attached to every message.
- **Background Operation** — Stays active with mic and TTS even when you switch apps.

### Requirements

- iPhone running **iOS 26+**
- A self-hosted [OpenClaw](https://github.com/openclaw) server
